How to make Virgin Strawberry Mojito

Ingredients

  • 6 fresh mint leaves
  • 2-3 strawberries
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon caster sugar / Honey
  • 2 tablespoon lemon juice/lime juice
  • Crushed ice
  • Sparkling water
  • Mint leaves and slice of lemon for garnishing

Preparation

  1. Put the mint leaves into a chilled glass and add the caster sugar and lemon juice
  2. Crushed the leaves with spoon (muddler) until the sugar is dissolved,
  3. then add strawberries and crush them a little bit more,
  4. Filled the glass with crushed ice and top up with sparkling water,
  5. Stir it gently and garnish it with mint leaves, strawberry and lemon wedges.
